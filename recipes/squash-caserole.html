Squash Caserole

Description

Tasty squash caserole with cheese and mushroom soup for a delicious thanksgiving meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound squash
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup mild cheddar cheese - grated

Instructions

  1. Cook squash and onion until tender.
  2. Drain.
  3. Add layers of squash, soup and cheese.
  4. Repeat.
  5. Top with bread crumbs.
  6. Bake 20 minutes at 375.
